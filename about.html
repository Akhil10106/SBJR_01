About Us

(SBJR - Suraj Bhan Jagdish Rai)

Welcome to Suraj Bhan Jagdish Rai (SBJR), your trusted agricultural shop since 2006, located on the busy National Highway, Ponta Sahib road in Chhachhrauli,
Yamuna Nagar District, Haryana. SBJR has been serving the farming community for nearly two decades, providing top-quality agricultural products and solutions that meet the diverse needs of farmers.

Founded by Gulshan Goel, a visionary in the agricultural sector, SBJR has grown from a small shop to a well-known destination for farmers in the region.
Mr. Goel's dedication and passion for agriculture have driven him to build a business that is not just about selling products but also about nurturing relationships
with farmers and supporting their growth. Under his leadership, SBJR has become synonymous with reliability, quality, and excellence. His deep understanding of
farming challenges and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Commitment to Quality

At SBJR, we believe that the success of our customers is our success. That's why we are committed to providing only the highest quality products to ensure the best results for our farmers.
